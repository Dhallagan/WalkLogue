## Description

Walk. Talk. Remember.

WalkLogue turns your daily walks into a journal. Hit record, lock your phone, and start walking. When you're done, your voice is transcribed into a written entry with your step count attached.

No typing. No staring at a blank page. Just talk.

VOICE JOURNALING
Record your thoughts while you walk. WalkLogue uses OpenAI Whisper to transcribe your voice into clean text. Lock your screen and keep talking, it records in the background.

STEP TRACKING
Connect Apple Health or Fitbit. Your steps show up on the home screen and get saved with every walk entry.

ASK YOUR JOURNAL
Search your memories by asking questions in plain English. "When was I at the sauna?" "What was I thinking about last Tuesday?" The AI reads your full journal to find the answer.

WEEKLY REFLECTIONS
Every Sunday, get an AI-generated summary of your week: what you wrote about, how many steps, what seems to be on your mind.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
All journal entries are stored locally on your iPhone. No cloud. No accounts. No tracking. Your journal never leaves your device. The only data sent externally is voice audio for transcription and entry text for AI features, processed by OpenAI and not stored.

DARK MODE
Journal at night. Light and dark themes that switch in one tap from Settings.

RETROACTIVE ENTRIES
Forgot to journal yesterday? Tap the empty day in your log and record a voice entry backdated to that date.

SEARCH
Full-text search across all your entries. Find any day, topic, or memory instantly.
